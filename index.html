Who We Are

Built on Bold Flavor.

Ted's doesn't dabble. We picked one thing, got obsessive about it, and never looked back. Every patty is thin, every press is intentional, every edge gets crispy on a screaming-hot flat-top griddle.

No trends. No shortcuts. No thick pub burgers with nowhere to hide. Just smashed-to-order beef, melty American cheese, and the kind of crunch you can hear from across the table.

Our Story

Named After Teddy.

Ted's started the way the best things do — with a name that meant something. Named after Teddy, a reminder that food should feel like family, not a transaction. No strangers here. Just people who came hungry and left regulars.

We're rooted in small-town Texas. Hico built us — the pace, the people, the honesty you only find where everybody knows your order before you say it. That spirit is pressed into every patty we put on the griddle.

This isn't a restaurant chasing a trend. It's a gathering spot. The griddle runs hot, the cheese stays melty, and the door's always open. Come as you are. Stay as long as you want.

Sit. Stay. Eat.
